Intro to Arctic Monkeys

Image source: Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys is a British indie rock band. Their nostalgic sound would appeal to fans of Kaiser Chiefs, Cage the Elephant or The Fratellis, but their lyrics, intricate and fast, are their biggest attraction. Their songs often focus on relationships and somewhat sleazy night club scenes. Earlier albums tended to be upbeat with contrastingly dark themes. In AM, their fifth and most popular album, influences from hip hop bled into their music, creating a new but still distinctly Monkeys-esque feel.
